# Email Style Guidelines

## Design Principles
- **Mobile-first**: Most emails are read on mobile devices
- **Scannable**: Use clear headings and bullet points
- **Action-oriented**: Make it easy to take action
- **Professional**: Maintain a business-appropriate tone
- **Accessible**: Use proper contrast and alt text

## Email Structure
1. **Subject Line**: Clear, specific, and actionable
2. **Header**: App branding and greeting
3. **Event Summary**: Key details in a scannable format
4. **Action Buttons**: Prominent RSVP options
5. **Additional Info**: Location, conference links, etc.
6. **Footer**: Contact info and unsubscribe

## Subject Line Guidelines
- **Be specific**: "Meeting Reminder: Team Standup - Tomorrow 9 AM"
- **Include urgency**: "URGENT: Client Call in 15 Minutes"
- **Use action words**: "Confirm", "Reminder", "Update"
- **Keep under 50 characters**: For mobile display

## Content Guidelines
- **Lead with the most important info**: Time, date, title
- **Use bullet points**: For easy scanning
- **Include context**: Why this meeting matters
- **Provide alternatives**: "If you can't attend, please reschedule"
- **Be concise**: Get to the point quickly

## HTML Best Practices
- **Responsive design**: Works on all screen sizes
- **High contrast**: Ensure readability
- **Large buttons**: Easy to tap on mobile
- **Fallback text**: For email clients that don't support HTML
- **Clean code**: Validate HTML and CSS

## Action Button Design
- **High contrast colors**: Green for confirm, yellow for reschedule, red for cancel
- **Large touch targets**: Minimum 44px height
- **Clear labels**: "Confirm Meeting", "Reschedule", "Cancel"
- **Prominent placement**: Above the fold when possible

## Accessibility Guidelines
- **Alt text**: For all images and icons
- **Semantic HTML**: Use proper heading structure
- **Color contrast**: Minimum 4.5:1 ratio
- **Keyboard navigation**: Ensure all actions are accessible
- **Screen reader friendly**: Test with assistive technology

## Compliance Notes
- **Include unsubscribe**: Required for commercial emails
- **Contact information**: Provide way to reach support
- **Privacy notice**: Brief mention of data usage
- **Opt-out instructions**: Clear process for stopping notifications
